PhishDetect

Thank you for joining our PhishDetect closed beta!

In order to provide a more secure service, and to conduct a more trusted beta program, we now require users to register for a secret token in order to access this service. A secret token is a unique string that is provided to you, after registration, in order to authenticate your access to the PhishDetect Node. Once your account is activated by the PhishDetect Node administrators you will be able to use the generated secret token with the PhishDetect browser extension.

The secret token does not have to be personal, and we welcome sharing the same code among colleagues. However, please do not share the secret token too widely or publish it. We will disable secret tokens we found being abused.

Please note: because we are currently manually vetting and activating registered accounts, we will prioritize requests from recognizable and verifiable individuals and organizations. Unverifiable accounts will most likely be rejected at this time.
